Clan Bot Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our bot.

Collection and Use

Clan Bot collects user ids, message ids, message content. We use user ids to moderate users. We use message ids to delete messages. Message content is for our "ghost pinging feature" we delete message content after 30 days and you may request that your data is deleted at any time.

Information Sharing

We will never sell or trade your data to anyone outside of Dever Systems.

Security

We keep all data secure no matter the cost, Dever Systems is commited to keep data safe from anyone who may try and breach our database. For any questions about our data protection please join discord.gg/alienclan and ping/dm Dever.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please join discord.gg/alienclan and ping/dm Dever.
